## In the future, i was still the same
___

[tThree hours earlier]

<p style="text-indent: 3em;">—I have been violated.</p>

Upon fully recovering his senses and analyzing his situation, he checked his body.

The same scrawny, useless hands, the same fragile body, and above his temples, the triangular ears that reminded him of what he should have been.

<p style="text-indent: 3em;">—So it turns out that either way, I still end up... Like this.</p>

This was Ciao, who had just experienced one of the worst disappointments in life: realizing that even after becoming an adult, he had not become stronger or at least less weak.

You could say his hair grew about thirty centimeters and his height one. After at least three years, those were all the differences between his current self and his past one, which in his case used to be his present.

And also his body had been abused, by himself.

In his own words, what had just happened was a violation. His consciousness had been forced into his future body. Something like time travel.

The problem here was that by jumping to the future, a large amount of memories were missing, being everything that his body had lived through during the skipped time, or rather, would have lived through.

Let alone the fact that he did not know who or why had caused this.

<p style="text-indent: 3em;">"Yes, yes, y-yes, I am coming!." He pleaded, covering his face— I shall do it as fast as I can, dang...</p>

Using the window frames as ladders, he tried to climb down from a house, as some people gathered to throw stones at him upon seeing him on the roof, next to an overturned water tank.

At the last part, he fell sitting down, leaving his clothes in a disgusting state, with a mixture of stagnant water and dirt.

There were about four people, all of them seemed foreign, which meant that in this case, Ciao was the foreigner here.

After lowering his head submissively and saying "yes" to all the warnings, they let him go. He didn not have the courage to object to explain his situation.

He thought he could not blame them. After all, seeing a man with long messy hair dressed in a military uniform, coming out of a container on your roof, was perhaps not the most comfortable scene one could witness.

However, he did not seem to have much responsibility over what had happened either. The poor man had only woken up a few minutes ago, with combat clothes, a body three to four years older than his mind, and trapped in some cylinder with water.

Since he had already attracted too much attention, he decided to walk wherever, to get away from the place. It was his first few minutes in the future and he had already publicly humiliated himself; now he could never return to that place again.

As he moved anywhere, he could form a basic theory of his current location and confirm that he was indeed in a time that was not his own.

<p align="center">❈❈❈❈❈❈❈❈</p>

According to the book "Hypothesis on the Future, the Past, and How to Reach Them" by the author Lo Mim; a personal locomotive without railway, or simply called an automobile, would be the invention of the century. And it seemed he had been right. Based on Mim's designs, Ciao had already counted fourteen automobiles, which were much better than what had been proposed.

Besides that, the public lighting that does not need fire, the utilitarian architecture, and even the individual cinematograph he found in his chest rig, were concepts similar to those suggested in the book, but much more refined.

Unfortunately, despite speaking about the future and time travel, the book did not offer any kind of guide on what to do in such cases.

Although he had read it long ago, he still remembered it clearly; that was Ciao's talent.

As for his location, leaving aside the obvious completely different layout; the weather was desert-like, it was curious to note that everyone else seemed to have less tolerance to heat than him; He also realized that the writing used different characters despite the fact that oral communication was possible and smooth.

That said, it was more than obvious that he was in a different country, however it was not easy to tell which one.
The reason being, that such a peculiar dude only received looks of disgust and was dismissed as crazy when he asked questions like "What country is this?" Or worse yet, "What year is it?".

Even finding himself in such a deplorable place, it was without a doubt the future. Even the piles of garbage were an undeniable indicator, plastics everywhere and an obvious planned obsolescence in all inorganic waste.

It was a marginal city, a failed attempt at urbanization that ended with most of the vegetation and turned the place into a garbage dump and construction remains. Decayed housing and ruins or unfinished constructions.

People walked completely used to it and therefore carelessly, the only different thing they had seen in years was Ciao himself.

Apparently he was the only one with animal characteristics, although he was not any better than a common human either.

He also had the palest skin in the entire region.

Based on the state of the place and his uniform, Ciao thought that perhaps...

<p style="text-indent: 3em;">"Come on, as if someone like me would participate in a war or something similar." He sighed after taking off his chest rig and jacket.<p>

Once he was far away enough so that no one would recognize him, his heart that seemed to be about to explode finally relaxed, likewise, the sunset was beginning.

Ciao had no currency or food of any kind. His equipment was limited to that strange black rectangle in his chest rig, and a gourd with little water.

At some point, no more people were visible around, not that he was lost, though. Even after moving so much without direction, he remembered perfectly every street and face he saw.

As he advanced through an alley, he began to hear some noises that seemed to be from some dog, or several. He did not give it much importance since besides being small, they are not usually aggressive, so he continued advancing through the same path, which brought him closer to the sound.

<p style="text-indent: 3em;">
"Come to think about it, it is not convenient to appear lost and defenseless, now is it?" Now more relaxed, he could analyze better; at first he had let himself be carried away by the desperation of the moment, but he should reduce all risks.</p>

Showing himself vulnerable in public would be the worst option. Besides, it was probable that time travel was not yet something common...

Then it could be heard that the animals charged towards him, he still could not see them but it was possible they were running at full speed, they began to bark, perhaps with hatred. Ciao paused briefly, they sounded too big.

—!!!

It was too late when he wanted to do something, four dogs triple the size of what he imagined, headed towards him with no intention of stopping.

Ciao ran with all his strength back, not without receiving two or three bites on his legs first.

A second later, he felt that he separated from the ground, he sighed mentally and closed his eyes trying to calm himself.

—Ah. Pox. These things always happen to me.

When he got out of the alley, something violently pushed him to the side, throwing him to the ground. By the sound, he knew that some heavy objects had fallen because of the impact, breaking.

Ciao curled up into a ball hugging his knees and closed his eyes, holding back the urge to scream and curse everything.

It was not that the fangs stabbing his body hurt, or that he had injured himself after hitting the ground, but rather that before falling, he saw a person.

That person, he was carrying some kind of cart with scrap metal and other stuff, that was what he crashed with.

<p style="text-indent: 3em;">"Yes! Apologies, f-forgive me. It was my fault." He stretched out his left arm without daring to raise his gaze.</p>

Drowned in shame and a new public humiliation, four beasts tried to tear him apart.

—At least here it was good for something not having a tail.

With his arms bleeding, Ciao did nothing more than squeeze his eyes shut to protect himself from the insults of that man, who would surely be upset about the damage to their merchandise.

He noticed it did not hurt as much as he had imagined, in fact he could say he was not feeling anything.

<p style="text-indent: 3em;">"Hey! Son of a... bitch"</p>

He heard, preparing himself to be hit, almost having forgotten that some dogs wanted to rip his ears off at that very moment.
